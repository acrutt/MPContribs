Apply for a Project

Fill out the form on the left to apply for a project on MPContribs. Upon submission of your info an email will be sent to an admin to approve your request. If approved, you can start uploading data to your project which will appear on the project landing page. You can choose to add e-mails of MPContribs users to your request who you like to be added to your project group as collaborators. Collaborators and owners have identical permissions but the number of projects an owner can create is limited. Your data will only be visible to you and your collaborators until you choose to make it public. Only public contributions will appear on the detail pages of Materials Project.
