Apply for a Project

Fill out the form on the left to apply for a project on MPContribs. Upon submission of your info an email will be sent to an admin to approve your request. If approved, you can start uploading data to your project which will appear on the project landing page. Your data will only be visible to you (and the collaborators you choose to add to the project) until you choose to make it public. Only public contributions will appear on the detail pages of Materials Project.
