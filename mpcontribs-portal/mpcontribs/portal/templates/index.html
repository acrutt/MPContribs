Community Contributions to Materials Project

MPContribs enables materials researchers to contribute experimental and theoretical materials data to Materials Project (MP). Data on MPContribs is collectively maintained as annotations to existing MP materials and automatically exposed to over 130,000 MP users. MPContribs disseminates contributions as preview cards on MP's materials details pages, in form of domain-specific interactive landing pages on this portal, and programmatically through its RESTful API. See the documentation for more info.
