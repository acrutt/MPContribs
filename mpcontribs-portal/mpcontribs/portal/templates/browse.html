Community Contributions to Materials Project

MPContribs enables materials researchers to contribute experimental and theoretical materials data to Materials Project (MP). Data on MPContribs is collectively maintained as annotations to existing MP materials and automatically exposed to over 190,000 MP users. MPContribs disseminates contributions as preview cards on MP's materials details pages, in the form of domain-specific interactive landing pages on this portal, and programmatically through a python client for its API.
