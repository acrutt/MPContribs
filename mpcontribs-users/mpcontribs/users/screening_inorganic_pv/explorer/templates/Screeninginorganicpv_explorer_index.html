Screening Inorganic PV

Mar 18 2018

Publication

We have screened ~33,000 non-magnetic insulators/semiconductors for which there was a computed bandstructure in the Materials Project for potential use as photovoltaic absorbers. Like many studies, we employ a "funnel" approach of increasingly stringent screening criteria that require increasingly expensive calculations. Below a summary of high-level parameters extracted from these data, all for ~800 compounds. Ultimately, the data we plan to share here are absorption coefficients (or full dielectric tensors), electronic densities-of-states (higher fidelity than the ones currently in the MP database), "corrected" bandgaps based on the method of Chan and Ceder (Phys. Rev. Lett. 105, 196403).
